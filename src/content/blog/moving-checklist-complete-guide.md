## 8 Weeks Before Your Move

- Research and shortlist packers and movers. Look for transparent, all-inclusive pricing and verified reviews.
- Call or WhatsApp PackersHub at +91 77310 74075 for a free survey and quote.
- Start decluttering — anything you haven't used in a year is worth reconsidering.
- Notify your employer of your change of address if needed for HR records.

## 6 Weeks Before

- Confirm your moving company and date. For interstate moves, 6 weeks provides comfortable crew and vehicle availability.
- Begin collecting packing materials or confirm that your movers will supply them.
- Notify your bank, insurance providers, and utility companies of your upcoming change of address.

## 4 Weeks Before

- Begin packing non-essential items: books, seasonal clothing, decorative items.
- Arrange transfer of school records if relocating with children.
- Contact your current and new housing societies about access restrictions, parking, and elevator bookings for the move.

## 2 Weeks Before

- Confirm all moving details with your packers and movers company.
- Pack the contents of your storage areas, garage, and attic.
- Arrange for utilities to be set up at your new address before moving day.

## Moving Week

- Defrost your refrigerator 24–48 hours before the move.
- Confirm your moving day schedule with the crew.
- Keep your documents, valuables, and medications with you personally — not in the truck.

## Moving Day

- Be present at both pickup and delivery to oversee loading and unloading.
- Check every item against the inventory sheet before signing off.
- Tip your crew generously if the job was well done — it matters more than people realise.

## After the Move

- Check all items against your inventory within 24 hours. Report any damage immediately.
- Update your address with voter ID, Aadhaar, and other official documents.
- Take a breath — the hard part is done.
