## South India's Relocation Economy

People move between South Indian states constantly — for IT jobs between Hyderabad and Bengaluru, for medical education between Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu, for manufacturing work between Karnataka and Kerala, for family reasons across all five states. PackersHub was built specifically for this geography.

## Route-Specific Considerations

**Hyderabad to Bengaluru:** The NH44 corridor is one of India's busiest interstate routes. PackersHub schedules these moves with early-morning departure to avoid the mid-day traffic concentration around Kurnool and Anantapur.

**Visakhapatnam to Chennai:** The NH16 coastal highway has seasonal restrictions in cyclone-prone months (October–December). Build in buffer time during this period.

**Thiruvananthapuram to Bengaluru:** Mountain terrain through the Ghats requires appropriate vehicle selection and more careful scheduling.

**Hyderabad to Chennai / Hyderabad to Pune:** Both high-volume corridors with PackersHub operating regular services.
